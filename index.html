Leap and Avoid

The ultimate test of timing, reflexes, and precision.

Welcome to the Challenge

Dive into a minimalist world where every move counts. Leap and Avoid is a pure, unadulterated skill-based game designed to push your reflexes to their limit. There are no complex stories or distracting power-ups—just you, the platform, and an endless series of obstacles. How long can you survive?

How to Play

The controls are deceptively simple, but mastering them is the key to a high score:

  • Move: Use the Arrow Keys or A/D keys to move your character left and right.
  • Jump: Press the Spacebar to perform a leap. Timing is critical to clear gaps and avoid hazards.

Your goal is to stay on the platforms for as long as possible. The game will get progressively faster and more challenging. Stay focused, anticipate your moves, and set a new record!

Core Features

  • Pure Skill Gameplay: No frills, just a raw test of your platforming abilities.
  • Instant Play: Built with HTML5 for immediate fun directly in your browser, with no downloads needed.
  • Minimalist Design: Clean visuals help you focus on the gameplay.
  • Endless Replayability: With a progressively increasing difficulty, you'll always have a new high score to chase.
